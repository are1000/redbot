Help

I don't want my messages to be transcribed by RedBot!
You can always opt-out by changing your RedBot policy to all.

What policies are there?
  • /policy all enables all policies - transcript will hide your identity.
  • /policy no-username toggles no-username policy - will replace all occurences of your username with REDACTED.
  • /policy none disables all policies - transcript won't hide your identity.
